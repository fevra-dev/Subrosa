This record is retained as historical context only. Key facts of the Decree-13 regime as sourced in `skills/data-minimization/references/regulatory-reference.md` §Vietnam: MPS enforcement; VND 5B administrative ceiling; basic/sensitive two-tier with explicit per-purpose consent; sensitive list including financial data and geolocation; Art. 26 in-country storage + MPS approval or self-assessment for export.

**Corrections discovered en route (2026-07-05, see reconciliation log):** the suite quick-select's "Art. 9 (minimization)" label was wrong — Decree 13's Art. 9 enumerated *data-subject rights*. Under the successor PDPL, Art. 9 = consent and rights sit at Art. 4. Consented processing under Decree 13 may continue without fresh consent (PDPL transition clause).
